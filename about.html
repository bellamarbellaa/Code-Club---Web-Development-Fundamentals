About Code Club

Discover More about Our Club....

Code Club was started by Greg Hamilton as a place for friends to gather together and have fun with code.
We meet at a top secret location once a month. Locations, dates and times are published here on this website,
in the members area 1 week before our next meeting.

Here are some things we love to work on:

  • Websites
  • Apps
  • Games (esp. retro games)
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Robotics
  • ...and also brewing the ultimate coffee blend :)
